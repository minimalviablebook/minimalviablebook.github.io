Site Launch!

Aug 12th, 2014

I have been writing a book http://slumsofpaloalto.com/. How I started, progressed, and finished is very different from how others think about books and publishing. It is insanely different. And insanely better. I hope describing it helps you.

I am mostly going to walk through chronologically because I think I generally did things in the right order. So each blog posting will be a replay of time.

An important thing to know about me is that I am a “startup specialist”. I launch companies and try to get them to a point where other people want to buy them or they can “go public” on stock market. This is the angle I am coming from. I am certainly a capable writer, but that is mostly technically oriented. And the book I wrote is an “augmented memoir”, which is a significantly easier thing to write than a novel or really any other form of book.

I can not help you be a great writer. I may be a horrible writer. But in a ridiculously short period of time, other people were reading my book. An actual book. Not a manuscript, draft, etc. It was a book.

That ridiculously short period of time from having never written a book to having a book that others are reading? One month! By two months I was at 150 pages and had mailed my book to Stephen Colbert, John Stewart, and Sheryl Sandberg. Plus dozens of people that I could easily reach in my home town. And the time-lines continue with that insane speed. Oh, and I was rejected by 42 literary agents in that same period. More than a dozen by the end of one month. So the time-lines and effectiveness are insanely different.

When normal is not giving you what you want, insanity (“think different”) can be insanely better!
